Before You Begin...!

A varietyb of living organisms are found on the earths. They may be bacteria,viruses,fungi,plants and animals.They may be microscopic or large. Bacteria,viruses, protozoa are some of the microscopic organisms.We cann't see microscopic organisms with our naked eyes. So we use a compound microscope to see them. The very small organisms which cann't seen with our naked eyes are called microscopic organisms. Most microscopic organisms are unicellular. In the unit, we will study about bacteria,viruses, fungi, modification of root,stem and leaf,seed and dispersal of seed; and life cycle of flowering plants.

Glossary:A dictionary of scientific/ techanical terms

primitive : very simple
microscopic: that can be seen only under microscope
cluster : a group of things of the same type that grow close together
metabolic : related to the chemical processes in living beings that change the food into energy and materials for growth
tendrils : a thin curling stem that grows from a climbing plant
embryo : very early stage of an organism
zygote : a diploid cell formed by the fusion of a male gamete and a female gamete
